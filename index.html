Our solution

Four pillars. One watchful eye.

CowCare runs on the IP cameras you already have. AI processes everything locally on an edge device — no wearables, no cloud uploads, GDPR-compliant by design.

Cow tracking with ID

Every cow gets a digital identity — recognised and tracked 24/7, purely by camera.

Behaviour recognition

Eating, drinking, lying, walking — AI spots the subtle changes that signal illness early.

Real-time alerts

Prioritised push notifications straight to the farmer's phone — act in minutes, not weeks.

Security detection

Recognises intruders — wolves, unknown people, threats — and warns you immediately.

An extra pair of eyes for every farmer.

How it works

From camera to
alert in three steps.

1

Your existing cameras

CowCare connects to standard IP cameras in the barn and on pasture — or we install them, bundled and financed.

2

AI on the farm, not in the cloud

Video is processed locally on an NVIDIA Jetson edge device. Raw footage never leaves the farm and is deleted after 14–30 days.

3

Alerts to your pocket

Behavioural anomalies become prioritised push alerts on your phone — up to 48 hours before you'd spot the disease yourself.

Zero sensors, collars or ear tags on the animal
